## Decisions Made

- Extended appContext beyond plan spec (`{ config, requireLocalOrApiKey }`) to include `log`, `debug`, `dataRoot`, `logDir`, `toolCallHandler` — routers need them for logging, build registry, logs endpoint, and chat tool-call loop
- `getCachedProjectPrompt` and its globals moved to routes/chat.js where they belong — they were only ever used by the chat handler
- `resolveFolder`/`findFolderByName`/`FOLDER_SEARCH_SKIP` duplicated in routes/config.js and routes/files.js — both genuinely need them; acceptable duplication for this refactor
- Build route inline rate limiters (next-action, research, plan) were `app.use()` calls inline before the handlers in the old server.js — moved to server.js app.use() section
- `GET /api/logs` placed in routes/git.js per plan spec (grouped with local VCS tooling)
